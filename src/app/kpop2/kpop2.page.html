Mamamoo

Introduction to BLACKPINK

MAMAMOO (마마무) consist of 4 members: Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein and Hwasa. MAMAMOO deuted in 2014, under Rainbow Bridge World (formerly WA Entertainment).

  • Agency: Rainbow Bridge World
  • Debut: Jun 18, 2014
  • Years Active: 2014–present
  • Most Popular Member: Moonbyul
  • Fandom: MooMoo

Members

Solar

(Kim Yong Sun) Solar is a member of the girl group MAMAMOO. Solar was on “We Got Married” where she was paired with Eric Nam and also appeared on “King of Masked Singer” as Sunflower.

  • Height: 5'4" (163cm)
  • Date of Birth: 28 years old, born February 21, 1991
  • Position: Leader, Main Vocalist, Visual, Face of the Group

Wheein

(Jung Whee in) Wheein is a member of the girl group MAMAMOO. Wheein appeared on “King of Masked Singer” as Half moon and she is a former MBK Entertainment trainee.

  • Height: 5'3" (159cm)
  • Date of Birth: 24 years old, born April 17, 1995
  • Position: Lead Vocalist, Lead Dancer

Hwasa

(Ahn Hye Jin) Hwasa is a member of the girl group MAMAMOO. Hwasa appeared on “King of Masked Singer” as Aerobic girl.

  • Height: 5'3" (160cm)
  • Date of Birth: 23 years old, born July 23, 1995
  • Position: Lead Vocalist, Lead Rapper, Maknae

Moonbyul

(Moon Byul Yi) Moonbyul is a member of the girl group MAMAMOO. Moonbyul was, alongside other 6 female idols, in “Idol Drama Operation Team” tv program. They created a 7 members girl group, called Girls Next Door , which debuted on July 14, 2017.

  • Height: 5'5" (165cm)
  • Date of Birth: 26 years old, born December 22, 1992
  • Position: Main Rapper, Main Dancer, Vocalist
