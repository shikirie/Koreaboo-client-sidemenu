Red Velvet

Introduction to Red Velvet

Red Velvet is a girl group composed of 5 members formed by S.M. Entertainment. The group debuted on August 1, 2014, with their digital single "Happiness". In March 2015 Red velvet added a 5th member to the group, Yeri.

  • Agency: SM Entertainment
  • Debut: Aug 1, 2014
  • Years Active: 2014-current
  • Most Popular Member: Irene
  • Fandom: ReVeluv

Members

Seulgi

Seulgi (Korean name: Kang Seul-gi; Hangul: 강슬기, born on February 10, 1994) is a South-Korean singer and dancer. She is the lead vocalist, the main dancer, and known as the face of the South-Korean girl group Red Velvet.

  • Height: 5'4" (162cm)
  • Date of Birth: 25 years old, born February 10, 1994
  • Blood Type: A
  • Position: Lead Vocal, Main Dancer

Irene

Bae Ju-hyun better known by her stage name Irene, is a South Korean singer, television host and actress. She is a member and leader of the South Korean girl group Red Velvet. She was part of SM Rookies.

Yeri

Kim Yerim (Hangul: 김예림, born March 5, 1999), also known as Yeri, is a South-Korean singer and dancer. She is the maknae, vocalist and sub rapper of the South- Korean girl group Red Velvet and was introduced to the group as a new addition on March 10, 2015.

  • Height: 5'2" (158cm)
  • Date of Birth: 20 years old, born March 5, 1999
  • Blood Type: O
  • Position: Vocalist, Rapper, Maknae
